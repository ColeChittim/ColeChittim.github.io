Werepigs in Space

You are one of the few who have been selected and deemed capable of this space exploration mission! After your spaceship collides with theirs, you must explore endless rooms of mystery! Are you up to the challenge in this exciting arcade game filled with turn based strategy?

Werepigs in Space was made using Unity 3D and released on Google Play and within the first week had been downloaded over 100 times with an overall rating of 4.7 stars.
