Description

Your assignment: Protect, Clean, and Discover. Collect resources and encounter the inhabitants of nearby galaxies as you also fill your home planet with alien plants, futuristic tools, and uncover the greatest threat to the universe.

Tiny Planet Protectors is a Free To Play game that allows all players to try out the game by exploring the Hearth Galaxy but all other and future galaxies are available to explore through paid DLC.

History

Tiny Planet Protectors is a project being developed by two people with a passion for making games they dream of playing. Cole creates the art and handles design and programming while Willa composes and records all the music as well as crafts the minimalistic and inviting soundscapes to explore. This will be their second project together and can’t wait for others to get lost in the universe, protecting every tiny planet.

Features

  • Explore a procedurally generated universe filled with endless planets across multiple galaxies!
  • Complete many relaxing cleaning activities as you travel the galaxy!
  • Farm, craft, and shape your home planet!
  • Uncover the greatest threat to the universe!
