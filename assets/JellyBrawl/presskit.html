Description

Jelly Brawl is a physics based local multiplayer party game where you gotta jiggle, flop, and pop your way to the top! Fight alone or against friends with unique jelly based combat and experience the gelatinous chaos across tons of stages, mini games, and modes as you struggle to be the last jelly standing!

History

Inspired by hours of yelling and throwing controllers in his parents basement, developer Cole Chittim wanted to make a local multiplayer game that would make him and his friends proud. Jelly Brawl aims to recapture the magic and madness of the fast paced couch multiplayer games of the 2000's and combine it with new emergent and wild physics based gameplay. We all have different moments in gaming we look back on fondly and for Cole, those hours of Wario Ware, Mario Party, and Gang Beasts have been his biggest inspiration for Jelly Brawl.
