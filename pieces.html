Picking Up The Pieces

I thought I understood my brain better than this.
But now I feel like I never did.



Picking up the pieces is an experimental game about digging through thoughts and striving to feel good enough when you feel alone. Explore a handful of levels with surreal visuals in this fully voiced adventure.
