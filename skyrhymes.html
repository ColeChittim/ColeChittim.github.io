Skyrhymes

Created as part of WWU Game Design Club's Fall Game Jam in 2018, Skyrhymes is a rap battle simulator set in the elder scrolls universe. I worked amongst a team of 4 and was responsible for all artwork, UI, and some of the gameplay programming. The entirety of the project was created in under 48 hours using the Unity3D engine.

Contains: Strong language
