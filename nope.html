Nope

A hyper casual game about bouncing a little round friend around your
screen and collecting gems. With addictive tough as nails gameplay,
it's hard to put down.
Nope was built using the Unity Tiny framework and written in TypeScript.
Mobile app editions were packaged using the Cordova framework

What started as a side project became a fun journey recreating the magic of
comparing scores with friends in games like Flappy Bird or Don't Touch the Spikes.
Within Nope's first week of being on the Google Play Store, Nope accumulated almost 500 downloads!
